EZPrint

In today's digital world, everything needs to be fast and easy—including printing. At Kolej Tun Dr. Ismail (KTDI), the current printing system is old and hard to use, causing problems for students and staff. That's why our team created EZPrint—a mobile app that makes printing easier. With EZPrint, users can upload, edit, and order documents from local vendors through a simple and friendly interface. It's made for students and staff who want a faster, more convenient way to print.

HydraMate

Nowadays, people care about health and convenience, but things like drinking enough water or knowing what's in your drink can still be hard. These small problems can affect your health and energy. That's why we created the HydraMate App—a smart and easy way to stay healthy. It helps you track your water intake, gives info about your drinks, and sends reminders to keep you on track.
